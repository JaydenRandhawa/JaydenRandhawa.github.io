Electrician

By: Jayden

Duties and Responsibilities

Electricians inspect, test, repair, install, and modify circuits in residential and commerical buildings, and on larger electrical grids.

Work Hours and Expectations

Electricians generally work a standard 8 hour day from
9AM - 5PM
Monday - Friday.

Clients expect electricians to available on short notice as they are often called when a problem needs to be solved quickly.

    Certification Process

      1. Apprenticeship
      The apprenticeship training program involves 4 8-week periods of technical training in addition to 8, 000 hours of working time.
      2. Certification
      To become certified, an apprentice must complete the 4-5 year training program and then acheive a 70% or higher on the final exam.
      3. Interprovincial Exam
      After earning their certification, an electrician can choose to take the interprovincial exam to earn their Interprovincial Standards' Read Seal which certifies an electrician in every province.
