Electrician

By: Jayden

Duties and Responsibilities

Electricians inspect, test, repair, install, and modify circuits in residential and commerical buildings, and on larger electrical grids.

Work Hours and Expectations

Electricians generally work a standard 8 hour day from
9AM - 5PM
Monday - Friday.

Clients expect electricians to available on short notice as they are often called when a problem needs to be solved quickly.

Environment and Conditions

Lineworkers

  • Construction
    • ○ Stays at each job for longer

  • Works Outdoors

  • Work destinations are predictable
  • Very dangerous

    Skills Required

      Dexterity
      Electricians often have to handle small wires and work in tight spaces so they must have good fine motor skills.





      Problem Solving
      Electricians often have to solve issues that dont have obvious solutions making problem solving an extremely important skill.
      Vision
      Electricians often have to work with limited light and with colour-coded components which requires good vision.
